The main ALC portal above was released in February, 2007. New materials are constantly added to this site by literacy scholars and authors who scan journals, attend professional society meetings, and review international resources related to all aspects of English literacy. This site includes audio files, opinion papers, bibliographies, videos, grant agencies, volunteer groups, ESL, and software. Recommendations for the addition of new concepts or resources may be sent by email to President@AmericanLiteracy.com.

 

The archived ALC web site above was active from 1990 through 2006. Materials that had continuing usefulness were moved to the new ALC portal site after review, editing, and updating. These older materials may be of interest to persons who had come to rely on various materials and historic resources that were posted there.
