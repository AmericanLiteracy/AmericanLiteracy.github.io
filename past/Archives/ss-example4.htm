 Sound-Spel Examples from the "Double-Line" program.

This example shows output from the "Double-Line" program which is available as a teaching tool here on this web site.

A parent or teacher would type in any text into Mocrosoft WORD (tm) for example, and then press a button to transliterate the text into a version of simplified spelling.

Many learners will be able to read and pronounce the second lines under each bold line. The dimmed line presents the words phonetically and persons who are confused by the arbitrary spellings in the bold line can at least pronounce the sounds of the words in the secondary lines.
