Welcome from the President!


For more than 150 years ALC has issued scholarly research, publications and innovative strategies, to help counter the dismal literacy statistics found in most English-speaking countries. Our goal is to share with you tools, concepts, and action plans that support this effort.

On this site we've collected a variety of related materials: scholarly articles, practical "how-to" case studies, recordings, videos, conceptual debates, sources for software packages, links to classroom materials, lists of funding sources, professional organizations, statistical studies, and examples of innovative initiatives.

ALC’s main purpose is to convey information on new solutions, innovative technologies, and tools for engaging more boldly in the battle for literacy. I can be reached here at the Boulder, Colorado literacy research center: PresidentALC2@AmericanLiteracy.com

Warm Regards, Robert Alan Mole,
President
