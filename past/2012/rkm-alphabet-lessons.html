RKM Alphabet

Introduction

The RKM Alphabet prepares students to make the transition from reading the English alphabet, through Doubleline, and eventually reverses the process.

These lessons can be used by both beginning readers and readers who are challenged learners that are unable to process the over 425 ways the English alphabet forms words.
